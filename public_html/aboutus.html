About VideoKID

What exactly are we?

What is it?

VideoKID is an online gaming market platform where gamers can buy, sell, or trade games with other members. Currently, we have over 120,000 active members using our marketplace.

VideoKID partners up with popular existing gaming hub such as Steam, Origin, and etc, to bring gamers from all platform together to trade, buy, and sell their already owned hims. Members can link their accounts with third-party clients to connect their online game library and use our website as a global digital gaming marketplace. Money earned through transactions with other members are automatically deposited into your PayPal account. VideoKID does not allow users to store and place currency on the web. The only option to access payment is through PayPal.

Although we market ourselves as a global gaming marketplace, we have a zero policy when it comes to dealing with physical items. VideoKID only handles with digital products. We hold zero responsibility when members are trading items using physical assets.
